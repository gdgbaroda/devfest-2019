About the communities

Google Developers Groups
GDG stands for Google Developers Group, a flagship community which is open for all. A GDG can take many forms — from just a few people getting together to large gatherings with demos and lectures, to events like code sprints and hackathons. See More >
Women Techmakers
WTM stands for Women Techmakers program, a global initiative by Google to provide visibility, community & resources to women in technology. See More >
GDG Baroda & WTM Baroda exist since 2013, one of the earliest in India & worldwide Google developer community space.












DevFest (Developers’ Festival) is annual event series organized by GDG chapters across the globe. Inspired by & uniquely tailored to the needs of the local community & region.

Focused on community building, learning about tech & non-tech advancements, DevFest can take many forms: speaker sessions, hands-on workshops, design sprints, hackathons, fun activities, networking & much more! Learn more about DevFest events >
